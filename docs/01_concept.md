# Glückritter Inc — Game Concept

## Pitch

You are not the hero. You are the *business* behind the hero.

Glückritter Inc is a browser-based management game inspired by tabletop RPGs. You run a mercenary company that recruits low-level adventurers, trains and equips them, brokers contracts with shady clients, and sends parties into dungeons — all in the name of profit.

Morality is optional. Competence is mandatory.

## Core Fantasy

- The feeling of being a D&D dungeon master who is also the party's sleazy accountant
- Watching a ragtag band of rookies slowly become a reliable crew you've invested in
- Weighing risk vs. reward on every contract: will this dungeon pay enough to cover the healer's hospital bill?
- The cold satisfaction of a clean, profitable run

## Tone

Dark-comic. Think *Blackadder* meets *Dungeon Keeper* meets a spreadsheet. The world has real stakes and violence, but the perspective is mercenary, not heroic. Heroes complain about wages. Clients have dubious motives. Dungeons are dangerous and procedurally indifferent.

## Win / Lose Conditions

There is no single win state — the game is an ongoing operation. You succeed by staying solvent and growing your reputation. You lose by going bankrupt, losing your licence (reputation hits zero), or having every hero dead with no funds to replace them.

Optional campaign mode: reach a target net worth within a time limit, or complete a chain of story contracts.


## Platform

Browser-only, no install. Single-player. Runs entirely client-side (no server required for the core game). Save state persists via localStorage.
